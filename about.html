Origin Story

H-Thai-ML started in the exciting center of Las Vegas, influenced by the city's lively atmosphere and wide range of food options, bringing together Thai flavors in an exciting way right in the heart of the bustling Strip. Its founders wanted to mix the thrill of Las Vegas with the deliciousness of Thai cuisine, creating a unique dining experience.
